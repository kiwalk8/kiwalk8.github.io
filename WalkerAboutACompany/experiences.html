Experience the Café

Play Pen

Guests are welcome to book time with our cats without the purchase of a drink. Prices are based on time:

  • 15 minutes: $10
  • 30 minutes: $20
  • 45 minutes: $40


Coffee Corner

With the purchase of time in the Play Pen, guests are provided a complimentary beverage of choice. Additional drinks can be purchased for $5.



Rules

You are a guest in our cats' home. Please be respectful by following these rules:

  1. Be Kind
  2. Be Gentle
  3. Have Fun


Adopt a Café Cat

Should you find your newest family member at Tucker's Cat Café, we will be delighted to help you through the adoption process! Please see an associate at the front counter for an adoption application or send us an email.
